# Team

## Contributing

If you would like to join the team or contribute in any way (documentation, coding, outreach, governance), please reach out through the [contact page](contact.md) or open an issue on GitHub.  We especially encourage participation from:

- Carriers and TPAs
- Brokers and benefits consultants
- Employers and HR technology vendors
- Developers working on insurtech platforms

Together we can create a healthier and more transparent benefits ecosystem.
